# Assess Influencer: {influencer_id}

## Context
**ACT AS:** Risk Analyst (/analyst)
**Goal:** Determine the impact of an Influencer on our Ends and Means.

## Instructions
1.  Read the Influencer artifact.
2.  **Judge Impact:** Is this a Strength, Weakness, Opportunity, or Threat?
3.  **Map to Elements:**
    *   Does it put a Goal at risk?
    *   Does it enable a Strategy?
4.  Create an **Assessment** artifact.

## Verification
- [ ] Is the judgment clear (Positive/Negative)?
- [ ] Are affected Ends/Means identified?
